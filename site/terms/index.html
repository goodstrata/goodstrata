1. The service

GoodStrata is software that helps run the day-to-day administration of an owners corporation (also called a body corporate or strata company): issuing and tracking levies, matching payments, following up arrears, triaging maintenance and dispatching contractors, sending meeting notices, drafting minutes, holding records, and maintaining an append-only audit log of what happened. Parts of this work are performed by automated software agents. What the agents may and may not do is described in clause 4.

We provide the service to owners corporations, their committees, their appointed managers, and individual lot owners and residents who are given access. We may add, change, or remove features over time. Where a change materially reduces the core functionality you rely on, we will give reasonable notice through the platform or by email.

GoodStrata is free for owners corporations to use. There is no per-lot software licence fee and no subscription to run your building on the platform. We sustain the business in two ways, both designed to be transparent and to keep our incentives aligned with owners:

4. Decisions are human; money is deterministic code

This is the heart of how GoodStrata works, and it is a term of your use, not just a design note.

  • Agents execute; humans decide. Software agents can prepare, propose, and carry out routine operational work, and they can assemble a decision for you with the evidence attached. They cannot make a decision that spends the owners corporation's money, commits the building, commences action against an owner, or bends a rule. Anything of that kind waits for an authorised person to approve, vote, or escalate.
  • The money is code, not the AI. Financial calculations — levy apportionment by entitlement, penalty interest, payment matching, fund splits, and vote weighting — are performed by deterministic, tested code, not by an AI model. The model may write covering words around a figure, but the figure itself is computed and recorded by the engine.
  • Everything is on the record. Actions are written to an append-only audit log that is designed so entries cannot be edited or deleted after the fact, and human actions are distinguished from agent actions.

Because software agents assist with drafting and triage, automated output can still contain errors or omissions. You remain responsible for reviewing anything material before you act on it, and for the decisions you approve. GoodStrata is a tool to help you administer your owners corporation; it is not a substitute for legal, financial, tax, or professional advice, and nothing it produces is such advice.
