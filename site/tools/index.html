Ask Claude about your building

GoodStrata plugs into Claude (and other AI assistants that support connectors) as a secure connector. Add it once, sign in with your GoodStrata account, and just ask: no dashboards, no exports. It reads your live building data and answers in plain English.

  1. In Claude, open Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector and paste the GoodStrata URL:
    https://mcp.goodstrata.com.au/mcp
  2. When Claude opens a GoodStrata sign-in window, sign in with your GoodStrata account and approve access.
  3. That's it. Just ask:
    "Give me a briefing on my building." "What needs my attention this week?" "What's our financial position? Any lots in arrears?"

Read-only for now. The connector can read your building, but it can't change anything, approve decisions, or move money. Those always stay a human tap inside the app. It uses the same sign-in and the same permissions you already have.

What am I paying my strata manager?

Drop in your AGM papers, budget, or a photo of your financial statement, and we'll read the numbers and show you (in plain dollars) what your manager charges each year. Free, and we don't store your document.
